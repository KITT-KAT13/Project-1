Today, we are celebrating mothers, for their hard work and dedication. And as today is mothers and here is a poem about mothers
A mother's love, a steady flame, A heart that calls you by your name. Through every storm and every trial, She walks beside you, mile by mile.

Her hands, though worn, are soft and kind, With every gesture, peace you'll find. She’s the warmth beneath the skies, The sparkle in a child's eyes.

She whispers wisdom, soft but true, A beacon that will guide you through. In moments quiet, or when loud, She’s the shelter in the crowd. Her laughter, like a song so sweet, A rhythm to which hearts will beat. In every hug, in every kiss, She gifts a world of endless bliss. For in a mother's heart, you see, A love that’s deep, a love that’s free. She’s the first to hold you near, A bond that time can’t interfere. To all the mothers, near and far, You shine like every guiding star. Your love, a light that always shows, In your embrace, the world still grows.

We love you mothers everywhere
